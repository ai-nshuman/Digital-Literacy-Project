5 Do's and 5 Don'ts for responsible social media use as a college student

Do’s

✔ Think before you post

✔ Use strong passwords and enable privacy settings

✔ Share accurate and verified information

✔ Respect others’ opinions and be polite

✔ Use social media for learning and networking

Don’ts

✘ Don’t share personal or sensitive information

✘ Don’t spread fake news or rumors

✘ Don’t engage in cyberbullying or negative comments

✘ Don’t click on suspicious links or unknown messages

✘ Don’t spend excessive time on social media
