## 🎯 About This Project

This repository is my **Digital Literacy Portfolio**, submitted as part of the CSE0001 course at VIT Bhopal University. The project is built around the scenario of being selected as a **Student Digital Ambassador** — helping peers navigate the digital world safely, professionally, and effectively.

The portfolio is structured across **five tasks**, each aligned with a module of the course, covering digital content creation, professional online presence, coding platforms, email communication, and cybercrime awareness.

> 💡 I hope to learn the fundamentals of AI/ML, build real projects, and grow a professional digital presence that reflects my journey as a Computer Science student.

## 📋 Module Summaries

### ✅ Task 1 — Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
Created a one-page infographic using **Canva** covering: what digital literacy is, safe internet practices, and tips for building a professional online presence. Exported as PNG and added to `task-1-presentation/`.

### ✅ Task 2 — Student Digital Portfolio
Set up accounts on **GitHub**, **LinkedIn**, and **Kaggle**. Created a GitHub profile README, filled in LinkedIn Education details, and set up a Kaggle profile for future data science practice. Screenshots in `task-2-portfolio/`.

### ✅ Task 3 — Coding & Collaboration Platforms
Completed the **HackerRank** 'Solve Me First' challenge and Python Introduction challenge. Built a 5-question **Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz** using Google Forms, linked to a Google Sheet for response tracking. Resources in `task-3-platforms/`.

**🔗 Digital Literacy Quiz:** [Click here to take the quiz](#) *(add your Google Form link here)*

### ✅ Task 4 — Professional Email Etiquette
Drafted two professional emails (assignment extension request + internship inquiry) and created a Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist. Wrote a reflection on a real-world poor digital communication scenario. Files in `task-4-email-etiquette/`.

### ✅ Task 5 — Cybercrime Awareness: UPI & Online Payment Fraud
Researched UPI/online payment fraud — how it works, who it targets, and its consequences. Produced a 'Stay Safe Online' prevention checklist with 8+ actionable tips specific to college students in India, including UPI safety and reporting channels. Files in `task-5-cybercrime/`.

**🚨 Report cybercrime:** [cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in) | Helpline: **1930**
